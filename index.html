Golden Hamster

The Syrian, or Golden hamster is the most common of the hamster species kept as a pet and comes in a variety of different coat types, colors and patterns.

  • Scientific Name: Mesocricetus auratus
  • Average Length: 6 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 3 years
  • Habitat: In the wild, they like to live in warm, dry areas

The golden hamster has expandable cheek pouches, which extend from its cheeks to its shoulders. They use their cheek pouches to transport food to their burrows. The word 'hamster' comes from the German word 'hamstern' which means 'to hoard'.
